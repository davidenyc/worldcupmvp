## FAQ (24 questions — answered like humans)

**1. Can I cancel any time?**
Yes. One click in your account, no chat, no retention dance. We'll keep you on through the period you've already paid for.

**2. Do you offer refunds?**
Yes — 7 days, full refund, no questions. Reply to any GameDay Map email and we'll process it.

**3. What's the difference between Fan Pass and Elite?**
Fan Pass is everything you need to find and book your watch spots. Elite is for people coordinating groups of 6+, planning across cities, or who want a human handling reservation logistics during the tournament. If you're the friend who picks the bar, Fan Pass. If you're the friend who plans the trip, Elite.

**4. Will my saves transfer if I upgrade or downgrade?**
Yes. Your saves are yours regardless of tier. If you downgrade past the free limit, we keep them but show only the most recent 5 until you upgrade again.

**5. Do I need an account to use the free tier?**
No. You can browse, filter (up to 2 countries), and view venue details without an account. Saves and alerts need an account.

**6. What countries are covered?**
All 48 nations qualifying for World Cup 2026. Filtering by any of them is included in Fan Pass and Elite.

**7. What cities are covered?**
All 17 host cities — New York, Los Angeles, Dallas, San Francisco, Miami, Seattle, Boston, Philadelphia, Kansas City, Atlanta, Houston, Las Vegas, Toronto, Vancouver, Mexico City, Guadalajara, Monterrey. Plus a handful of major non-host cities with strong supporter scenes.

**8. How many venues are on GameDay Map?**
We launch with several hundred curated venues across NYC and a growing list across the other 16 cities. New venues land daily — we'd rather ship a smaller list of real spots than a huge list of generic results.

**9. Where does the venue data come from?**
A combination of our own sourcing, partnerships with bars and supporter clubs, community submissions, and verified public sources. Every venue is human-reviewed before it appears.

**10. How are venues ranked?**
By a combination of supporter affiliation, atmosphere score, capacity, reservation availability, and recent reviews. Fan Pass shows the full ranking; free shows a curated subset.

**11. Can I submit my own bar?**
Yes — every visitor can. Use the Submit a Venue button. Free submissions are reviewed in 5–7 days; Elite members get 24-hour review.

**12. I own a bar. How do I get featured?**
Two options: (a) claim your existing listing for free at /partners and edit your details, photos, and matches, or (b) email partners@gamedaymap.com about Featured placement during the tournament — five spots per neighborhood.

**13. Does GameDay Map work outside the US, Canada, and Mexico?**
Yes. We focus on the 17 host cities, but we cover supporter scenes in major non-host cities too — London, Buenos Aires, São Paulo, Tokyo, Seoul, others. If yours is missing, submit it.

**14. Are reservations real or just a request form?**
Both. For partner venues, your reservation goes straight into their booking system. For non-partner venues, we send a request and confirm back to you. Elite's Concierge handles the back-and-forth either way.

**15. What does "Hot Spot" mean?**
It's our editorial pick for a given match — the room that nailed it last time the same matchup played, or the spot with the right crowd, capacity, and atmosphere for that exact game.

**16. Will I get spammed with emails?**
No. Match alerts only fire for matches you save. Tonight digests only fire on days with matches. Weekly previews are weekly. You can switch any of them off in account preferences without leaving the platform.

**17. Do you sell my data?**
No. We make money from subscriptions and partner placements, not from selling user data. See our privacy policy.

**18. Is there a student or group discount?**
Yes. Email students@gamedaymap.com from a .edu address for 50% off Fan Pass. Group discounts (4+) at hello@gamedaymap.com.

**19. Can I gift Fan Pass to a friend?**
Coming soon. Until then, the referral link in your account gives you both a free month if they upgrade.

**20. Does Fan Pass work after the World Cup ends?**
Yes — we'll be active during qualifiers, club competitions, and the next major tournaments. The "World Cup" framing is the launch hook, not the lifespan.

**21. What devices does the app run on?**
Any modern browser. Add to home screen on iOS/Android for a near-app experience. Native apps are on our roadmap.

**22. How do you handle bars that are full?**
Capacity signals come from venue staff and crowd reports during matches. For Elite members, our Concierge can hold space for groups of 6+ in advance.

**23. What's your refund and chargeback policy?**
7-day refund, no questions. After 7 days, prorated refunds for service issues only. Chargebacks without contacting support first are reviewed case-by-case.

**24. Where do I report a wrong listing or a bar that's closed?**
Every venue page has a "Report this listing" link in the footer. Or email hello@gamedaymap.com — we fix listings within 48 hours.
