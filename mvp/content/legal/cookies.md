# Cookie Notice

We use a small number of cookies and similar storage. Here's what they are and how to control them.

## Strictly necessary
- `gdm_session` — keeps you signed in.
- `gdm_csrf` — protects against cross-site request forgery on form posts.
- `gdm_theme` — remembers your dark/light preference.

These cannot be disabled in-product because the site doesn't work without them. You can clear them in your browser.

## Functional
- `gdm_city` — your selected home city, so the homepage and Tonight load with your context.
- `gdm_recent_filters` — last filter set on the map page so you don't redo it.

You can disable these in /account → preferences. The site still works; you'll just re-pick your city each visit.

## Analytics (consent required)
- PostHog cookies (`ph_*`) — pseudonymous usage analytics.

We do not enable analytics until you accept. Decline = analytics off. Accept = analytics on; you can revoke any time.

## Marketing
We don't run ad cookies. We don't sell your data.

## Controls

- In-product: cookie banner on first visit, plus /account → privacy.
- Browser: clear cookies any time.
- Global Privacy Control: we honor GPC headers as an opt-out signal.

Email privacy@gamedaymap.com with questions.
