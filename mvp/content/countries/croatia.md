# Croatia

Two World Cup finals in two recent cycles. Smaller diaspora than the football culture suggests, but tight-knit and fanatic. Modrić-led generation entering its sunset.

## How Croatia fans show up

Croatian-American social clubs, restaurants, and Croatian community centers are the home base. Pittsburgh's Croatian community is one of the oldest in the US. Toronto's West End Croatian community is similarly historic.

## Where to find the crowd

- **Pittsburgh:** Croatian-American social clubs.
- **Cleveland:** West Side Croatian neighborhood.
- **Chicago:** Croatian Cultural Center.
- **NYC:** small Croatian Cultural Center; Astoria spots.
- **Toronto:** Croatian community in West End and Mississauga.

## Match-day rituals

- Red-and-white checkerboard everywhere.
- "Lijepa naša domovino" (the anthem) sung loudly.
- Cevapi, sarma, peka.
- Quiet first half, explosive second half (Croatian pattern).

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Modrić's last World Cup.
- Group stage with England at AT&T Stadium — repeat of recent World Cup history.

Red and white checkered. Croatia shirt. Or red T-shirt.

Knowledge of Modrić's career. The room will appreciate it.
