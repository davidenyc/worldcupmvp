# Egypt

The Pharaohs return to the World Cup. The Egyptian-American community in Astoria (Queens), Bay Ridge (Brooklyn), Jersey City, and Philadelphia is enormous and devoted. Mohamed Salah pilgrimage rooms.

## How Egypt fans show up

Egyptian restaurants, shisha lounges, and Egyptian-Arab cafes are the home rooms. Steinway Street in Astoria is the densest concentration. Coffee, shisha, full attention to the screen.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC (Astoria):** Steinway Street ("Little Egypt") cafes and shisha lounges. Layali, Kabab Cafe.
- **NJ:** Jersey City and Paterson Egyptian-Arab restaurants.
- **Philadelphia:** Northeast Philly Egyptian-Coptic community.
- **LA:** Anaheim/OC Egyptian-American spots.
- **Houston:** small but growing scene.

## Match-day rituals

- Mint tea and Turkish coffee.
- Shisha (hookah).
- Arabic and English flowing simultaneously.
- Mohamed Salah jerseys everywhere.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- First WC appearance since 2018.
- Salah's last realistic shot.
- Group stage with Belgium at Lumen Field.

## What to wear

Red, white, black. Egypt shirt — Salah back included.

## Bring

Respect for the cafes. Don't expect alcohol; do expect tea, coffee, and shisha.
