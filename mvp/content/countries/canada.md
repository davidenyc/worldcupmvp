# Canada

Co-hosts. Canada's first WC since 1986 was 2022; this is the home tournament. Toronto and Vancouver bars will be national attention rooms. The Canadian Soccer Association supporter culture has grown enormously since 2022.

## How Canada fans show up

Pubs and sports bars across Canada will be packed for any Canada match. Liberty Village/King West in Toronto (BMO Field-adjacent), Yaletown/Gastown in Vancouver. Bar Hop, Mahony & Sons-style bars are typical home rooms.

## Where to find the crowd

- **Toronto:** Bar Hop, The Pour House, Library Square. King West and Liberty Village hub.
- **Vancouver:** The Pint, Library Square, Mahony & Sons.
- **Montreal (proxy):** McLean's Pub.
- **Buffalo, Detroit, Seattle (border-adjacent US cities):** Canadian-themed pubs and Canadian-American bars.
- **NYC:** small Canadian community; default to generic sports bars.

## Match-day rituals

- "Oh Canada" sung at full volume.
- Maple leaf flags.
- Polite cheering until a goal, then pandemonium.
- Beer and poutine.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Hosting matches at home, including BMO Field opener vs Bosnia.
- Alphonso Davies and Jonathan David lead the squad.
- Pressure of host status.

## What to wear

Red and white. Canada shirt. Maple leaf face paint optional but appreciated.

## Bring

National pride. Canada doesn't take qualification or hosting for granted.
