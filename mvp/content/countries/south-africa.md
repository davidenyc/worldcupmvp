# South Africa

Bafana Bafana. Returning to the WC after long absence. The South African-American community is small but tightly networked — multiple South African expat groups in NYC, LA, Atlanta.

## How South Africa fans show up

South African expat groups, occasional South African restaurants, and rugby pubs (which often default to South African football matches too) are home rooms.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** South African Cultural Center events; rugby pubs.
- **LA:** Manhattan Beach and Pasadena South African expat scenes.
- **Atlanta:** South African expat groups in Buckhead and Brookhaven.
- **Houston:** small but real.

## Match-day rituals

- Castle Lager or Black Label if available.
- Boerewors (South African sausage), biltong (jerky).
- Vuvuzelas (mercifully optional).
- Multi-cultural rooms (Bafana Bafana fans are diverse).

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Opening the tournament vs Mexico at the Azteca.
- First WC since 2010 (where they hosted).
- Group stage matches against Spain (Atlanta) and Czechia.

## What to wear

Green and gold. South Africa shirt — the iconic '96 design works too.

## Bring

A vuvuzela. Or don't. Read the room.
