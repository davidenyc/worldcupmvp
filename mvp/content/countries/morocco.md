# Morocco

Heroes of 2022. Africa's first World Cup semifinalist. The Moroccan-American community in NYC, Boston, DC and Moroccan-Canadian community in Montreal will be at full volume for any Morocco match — and shared rooms with Algerian and Tunisian fans for African solidarity.

## How Morocco fans show up

Moroccan restaurants, North African cafés, and shisha lounges are the home rooms. NYC's Moroccan-Arab cafés in Brooklyn and Astoria. Boston's North African community. Montreal's enormous Moroccan-Quebecois scene.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** North African cafes in Astoria; Moroccan restaurants in Brooklyn (Atlantic Ave area).
- **Boston:** Tangier, Allston-area Moroccan-Arab spots.
- **DC area:** Moroccan restaurants in NoVa.
- **Montreal:** Plateau Moroccan restaurants — the largest Moroccan community in NA.
- **Houston, LA:** smaller but real.

## Match-day rituals

- Mint tea and pastries.
- Shisha (hookah).
- Tagine and couscous.
- Red flags, "Dima Maghreb" chants.
- Drumming.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Defending semifinalists from 2022.
- Group stage with Brazil at MetLife — a marquee fixture and major NYC moment.

## What to wear

Red and green. Morocco shirt. Red dominant.

## Bring

Patience for the cafés. Shisha lounges have their own pace.
