# Paraguay

Returning to the World Cup. The Paraguayan diaspora is small but tight — mostly in Miami, NYC, LA. Often share rooms with Argentine and Uruguayan supporters.

## How Paraguay fans show up

Paraguayan restaurants and Latin American restaurants generally are the home rooms. Sopa paraguaya (cornbread, despite the name), chipa (cheese bread), tereré (cold mate).

## Where to find the crowd

- **Miami:** Paraguayan-Argentine crossover spots in Doral.
- **NYC:** small Paraguayan community in Queens.
- **LA:** small but real.

## Match-day rituals

- Tereré (cold mate, sometimes with herbs).
- Chipa (cheese bread).
- Quiet but loyal crowd.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- First WC since 2010.
- Group stage with USA at SoFi — Friday night opener.

## What to wear

Red, white, blue. Paraguay shirt.

## Bring

Patience for tereré (the slow-sip mate culture).
