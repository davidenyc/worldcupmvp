# Congo DR

Returning to the World Cup with a strong recent form. The Congolese diaspora is concentrated in DC, Atlanta, Houston, and Toronto — and Congolese rumba (soukous) is the soundtrack.

## How Congo DR fans show up

Congolese restaurants, churches, and rented community halls are the home rooms. Soukous music between halves. Kinshasa-style mass celebration energy.

## Where to find the crowd

- **DC area (proxy):** Congolese restaurants in Maryland and Virginia suburbs.
- **Atlanta:** Decatur, Clarkston refugee-resettlement community.
- **Houston:** Congolese churches and community halls in southwest Houston.
- **Toronto:** Congolese-Canadian community in North York and Scarborough.
- **NYC:** small but real Bronx community.

## Match-day rituals

- Soukous music, dancing.
- Multi-generational, family-oriented rooms.
- Liboke (banana-leaf-wrapped fish or chicken) when restaurants serve it.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Group stage with Portugal at NRG.
- Strong African qualifier campaign.

## What to wear

Sky blue, yellow, red. Congo DR shirt or scarf.

## Bring

Patience for the music. Soukous takes time to build.
