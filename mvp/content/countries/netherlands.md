# Netherlands

Oranje. Total football. Heineken-fueled supporter culture in orange jerseys. Dutch-American scene is small but the orange shows up in force for big matches.

## How Netherlands fans show up

Dutch-themed bars, beer halls with Dutch beer, and Dutch cultural centers are home rooms. NYC has multiple Dutch-themed spots. The Holland, MI Dutch-American community shows up at neighboring sports bars in proxy.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Dutch Fred's (Hell's Kitchen), The Dutch (Soho), Vandaag (East Village).
- **SF:** Pilsner Inn (Dutch-influenced).
- **LA:** small Dutch cultural community.
- **Toronto:** Bier Markt for Dutch beer; small Dutch-Canadian community.
- **Houston:** small.

## Match-day rituals

- Heineken pints (yes, the actual pints from the country).
- Bitterballen (Dutch fried meatballs).
- Stroopwafels for treats.
- Orange shirts head-to-toe.
- "Hup Holland Hup" chant.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- After 2022 quarterfinals exit to Argentina, redemption arc.
- Group stage with Japan at AT&T Stadium.

Orange. Netherlands shirt. Wooden shoes optional, ironic.

## Bring

Capacity for Heineken. Multiple rounds expected.
