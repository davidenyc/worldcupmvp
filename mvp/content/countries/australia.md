# Australia

The Socceroos travel well and the Aussie expat scene knows how to drink and chant. Pubs, beer, and the unofficial anthem "Stand Up Aussie." Loose, loud, lovable.

## How Australia fans show up

Aussie expat pubs in LA (Venice, Santa Monica), NYC (multiple Aussie-American pubs), and SF (Sutter Station) are the home rooms. Beer is served in pints; chants are loud; the room takes a long time to settle but never quiets.

## Where to find the crowd

- **LA:** Cock 'n Bull, The Surly Goat, Cheekys Pub.
- **NYC:** The Australian (Midtown — appropriate name), Sydney's, Eight Mile Creek.
- **SF Bay:** Sutter Station, Rose & Crown.
- **Toronto / Vancouver:** Aussie-themed pubs in West End and Yaletown respectively.
- **Seattle:** Aussie expats default to UK pubs.

## Match-day rituals

- Beer. Specifically VB or Coopers if you can get it.
- "Aussie Aussie Aussie" / "Oi Oi Oi" call and response.
- Lamb pies if the kitchen has them.
- Greenstreet end-zone-style celebrations.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- After making knockouts in 2022, AUS will be confident.
- Played the USA in group stage at Lumen Field — a rare regional fixture.

## What to wear

Gold and green. Socceroos shirt. Aussie flag bandanas common.

## Bring

Patience for chants you don't know. Aussie pub crowds will explain after.
