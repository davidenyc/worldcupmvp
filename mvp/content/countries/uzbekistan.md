# Uzbekistan

A first-ever World Cup appearance. The Uzbek-American community is concentrated in Brooklyn (Rego Park, Kensington, Brighton Beach overlap with Russian/Bukharan Jewish community) and Sacramento. Central Asian restaurants — plov, samsa, kebabs — become event rooms.

## How Uzbekistan fans show up

Uzbek and Bukharan-Jewish restaurants are home rooms. Brooklyn's Rego Park has multiple options. Sacramento's Uzbek community is large for a smaller city.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC (Brooklyn):** Rego Park, Kensington Uzbek/Bukharan restaurants. Cheburechnaya, Salute, Nargis Cafe.
- **Sacramento (proxy):** Uzbek community is one of the largest in the US.
- **NJ:** small.

## Match-day rituals

- Plov (national rice dish), samsa, lagman.
- Chai (green tea, served constantly).
- Multi-generational rooms.
- Russian and Uzbek flowing.
