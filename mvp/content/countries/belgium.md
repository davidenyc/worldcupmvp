# Belgium

The Red Devils. Smaller fan presence in North America than Western European peers, but Belgian beer culture provides natural watch-party rooms.

## How Belgium fans show up

Belgian beer bars are the home base — wherever a venue specializes in Belgian beer (Trappists, lambics), Belgian fans congregate there for matches. Quiet, beer-focused, not chant-heavy.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Belgian Beer Bar (Manhattan), Vol de Nuit, Spuyten Duyvil (Williamsburg).
- **DC (proxy):** Brasserie Beck.
- **Boston:** Lord Hobo, Trillium Garden (Belgian-influenced).
- **Toronto:** Bier Markt locations carry Belgian beer; also occasional Belgian community events.

## Match-day rituals

- Belgian beer is the point. Trappist ales, witbier, sours.
- Frites with mayonnaise.
- Knowledge-driven conversation about the game.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- "Golden Generation" largely retired; new face emerging.
- Disappointment from 2022 group exit still fresh.

## What to wear

Red, yellow, black. Belgium shirt. Diagonal tricolor scarf.

## Bring

A willingness to drink expensive beer. The room won't push you to chant — just enjoy.
