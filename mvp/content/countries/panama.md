# Panama

The Canaleros. The Panamanian-American community in NYC's Brooklyn (Bedford-Stuyvesant, Crown Heights) and Miami's Allapattah is small but loud.

## How Panama fans show up

Panamanian restaurants, Latin American cafés, and Caribbean-Latin crossover spots are the home rooms. NYC's Crown Heights Panamanian community is one of the oldest in the US.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC (Brooklyn):** Panamanian restaurants in Crown Heights, Bed-Stuy.
- **Miami (Allapattah):** Panamanian restaurants and Latin Café crossovers.
- **Houston, DC:** small communities.

## Match-day rituals

- Sancocho (national soup) and rice with chicken.
- Reggae and salsa music between halves.
- Red, white, blue flags.
- Drumming.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Group stage with Ghana at BMO Field and England at MetLife.
- Strong CONCACAF qualifying form.

## What to wear

Red, white, blue. Panama shirt or scarf.

## Bring

Energy. Panama rooms are smaller but loud.
