# France

Reigning runner-up. Multilingual, multicultural supporter base reflecting the team itself. French expat scene is more sophisticated than chant-driven — bistros, brasseries, and wine.

## How France fans show up

French bistros and brasseries are home rooms. NYC's Le Jardin Bistro, Brasserie Cognac. Miami's Le Bouchon. LA's various French bistros. Senegalese-French fans share rooms with Senegalese fans for matches like France-Senegal.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Le Jardin Bistro, Brasserie Cognac (Midtown East), Lucien (East Village).
- **Miami:** Le Bouchon du Grove, La Sandwicherie.
- **LA:** Buvette, Petit Trois.
- **Toronto, Montreal:** French bistros and Quebec French bars.
- **Houston:** Brasserie 19, Etoile.

## Match-day rituals

- Rosé and red wine.
- Cheese plates between halves.
- "La Marseillaise" sung at the anthem.
- Knowledgeable football conversation.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Mbappé's tournament.
- Recovery from 2022 final loss.
- Group stage with Senegal at MetLife — major NYC diaspora moment.

## What to wear

Blue, white, red. France shirt. Béret optional but loved.

## Bring

A wine palate and a willingness to discuss tactics.
