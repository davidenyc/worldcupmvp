# Austria

Smaller diaspora, tight rooms. Austrian-American fans share German beer halls and Viennese cafés.

## How Austria fans show up

There aren't many dedicated Austrian bars. Austrian fans show up at German beer halls (Radegast, Heidelberg in NYC), Viennese-style cafés where they exist, or specific Austrian cultural centers in cities with Austrian-American populations.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Cafe Katja (LES), Edi & the Wolf. German beer halls accept Austrian flags.
- **Chicago:** Austrian cultural center.
- **Toronto:** German Club, occasional Austrian cultural events.
- **Other host cities:** likely a German beer hall by default.
