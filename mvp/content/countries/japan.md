# Japan

Samurai Blue. Disciplined, loud-when-it-matters supporter culture. Izakayas are the home rooms.

## How Japan fans show up

Izakayas (Japanese pubs) are the home base. NYC's East Village izakaya cluster, LA's Sawtelle and Little Tokyo, SF's Japantown, Seattle's CID. Sake, draft beer, edamame, full attention to the screen.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Sozai (Izakaya Ramen), Izakaya Juraku, Izakaya NoMad, Izakaya Zest. East Village cluster.
- **LA:** Sawtelle Japantown — Izakaya Bizan, Plan Check (LA-modern). Little Tokyo: Daikokuya, Honda-Ya.
- **SF:** Japantown — Izakaya Roku, Hapa Izakaya.
- **Seattle:** Maneki, Tsukushinbo, Utagawa.
- **Toronto:** Robson area Hapa Izakaya, Suika.
- **Vancouver:** Hapa Izakaya, Suika.
- **Houston:** Westchase / Town & Country izakayas.
- **Dallas:** Plano and Frisco Japanese restaurants.

## Match-day rituals

- Sake and draft beer (Sapporo, Asahi).
- Edamame, gyoza, takoyaki.
- Quiet between goals, explosive on goals.
- Synchronized cheers — "Nippon!" call and response.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- After upsets vs Germany and Spain in 2022, expectations are high.
- Group stage with Netherlands at AT&T Stadium.

## What to wear

Blue and white. Japan shirt — current or 2022 home kit.

## Bring

Patience. Japanese rooms are quiet between moments. The energy comes when it counts.
