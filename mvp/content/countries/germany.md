# Germany

Beer halls. Pretzels. Knowledgeable, methodical football culture. The German-American beer hall scene is one of the strongest natural supporter networks in North America.

## How Germany fans show up

German beer halls are the home rooms. Radegast (Williamsburg), Loreley (LES), Heidelberg (UES), Bierhaus (Midtown), Jägerhaus in NYC. Hofbräuhaus locations across many cities. Long tables, big beers, schnitzel.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Radegast Hall & Biergarten (Williamsburg), Loreley (LES), Heidelberg (UES), Bierhaus NYC, Jägerhaus, Zum Stammtisch (Glendale).
- **Chicago / Milwaukee (proxy):** German-American beer halls.
- **Houston:** King's Biergarten (Pearland), Spring Sausage.
- **LA:** Wirtshaus, Red Lion (Silver Lake).
- **SF:** Schroeder's, Suppenkuche.
- **Toronto:** Bier Markt, Otto's Bierhalle.
- **Atlanta:** Der Biergarten, Steinhaus.
- **Boston:** Bukowski Tavern (German-influenced).

## Match-day rituals

- Steins of Hofbräu, Paulaner, Spaten.
- Pretzels, schnitzel, sauerbraten.
- "Schwarz und weiß, wie liebe ich dich!" Limited chants but loud anthem.
- Lederhosen optional.
