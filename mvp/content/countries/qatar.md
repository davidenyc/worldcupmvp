# Qatar

Returning to the World Cup as host of 2022. Small Qatari diaspora in North America — mostly diplomatic, students, and business. Levantine and Arab restaurants will host.

## How Qatar fans show up

Qatari fans share Arab community rooms — Levantine restaurants, shisha lounges, Arab-American cafés. Mostly indistinguishable from broader Gulf Arab supporter rooms in NA.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Levantine, Arab cafés in Astoria.
- **DC area:** diplomatic community in NoVa.
- **Houston:** Arab-American community in west Houston.

## Match-day rituals

- Tea, Turkish coffee, Arabic coffee (gahwa).
- Mansaf, kabsa, and Arabic mezze.
- Shisha.
- Quiet, knowledgeable supporter base.

## Tournament storylines (2026)
