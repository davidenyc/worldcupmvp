# USA

Co-host. The American Outlaws (the supporter group) are well-organized across every host city. Sports bars across the US become USMNT rooms during matches; SoFi Stadium hosts the USA opener.

## How USA fans show up

Sports bars in every host city. Pioneer Square in Seattle (Sounders supporter culture overlap), Power & Light in KC, Pitch 25 in Houston, Pitch & Pint-style places everywhere. American Outlaws chapters organize watch parties in every major city.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** The U bar and grill, Dalton's, East End Bar & Grill, Football Factory, Smithfield Hall.
- **LA:** Cock 'n Bull, Sunset Junction sports bars.
- **SF:** Final Final, Kezar Pub.
- **Seattle:** Pioneer Square — Pyramid, FX McRory's, Fuel.
- **Houston:** Pitch 25, Lucky's Pub, Truck Yard.
- **Atlanta:** Brewhouse Cafe, Park Bar.
- **KC:** Power & Light District.
- **Toronto:** generic-good sports bars in King West.
- **Every other host city:** American Outlaws watch party at a designated bar.

## Match-day rituals

- "I Believe That We Will Win" chant.
- Beer (any American craft).
- Wings.
- Stars and Stripes face paint optional.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Co-host pressure.
- Pulisic, McKennie, Reyna core.
- Mauricio Pochettino as manager.
- Group stage with Paraguay at SoFi (opener) and Australia at Lumen Field.

## What to wear

Red, white, blue. USMNT shirt — current or any era. American Outlaws scarf.

## Bring

Energy. The USMNT supporter scene takes itself seriously.
