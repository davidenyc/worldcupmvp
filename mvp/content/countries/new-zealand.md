# New Zealand

The All Whites. Smaller diaspora than Australia, often share Aussie pubs in NA. Rugby pubs sometimes default to Kiwi matches.

## How New Zealand fans show up

Aussie expat pubs and rugby pubs are home rooms. Kiwi-American scene small but enthusiastic. LA's South Bay has a small concentration; NYC's a few Kiwi-themed spots.

## Where to find the crowd

- **LA:** Aussie pubs default; small Kiwi cluster in South Bay.
- **NYC:** Aussie-Kiwi pubs in Midtown.
- **SF:** Kiwi & Co (if still operating). Aussie pubs default.
- **Toronto:** Aussie pubs; small Kiwi community.

## Match-day rituals

- Speights, Steinlager, or Mac's beer if available.
- Rugby pub culture overlap.
- Quiet supporter base — knowledgeable, not chant-heavy.
