# Türkiye

Returning to the WC. The Turkish-American community in NYC's Sunnyside (Queens) and Brooklyn, plus Houston, is enthusiastic. Turkish coffee houses, kebab shops, and shisha lounges are home rooms.

## How Türkiye fans show up

Turkish restaurants, cafes, and shisha lounges. NYC's Sunnyside has a real Turkish corridor. Toronto has Turkish-Canadian community in Etobicoke.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC (Sunnyside, Astoria):** Turkish cafes and kebab houses on Queens Blvd.
- **Houston:** Turkish-American restaurants in Westchase.
- **Toronto:** Turkish-Canadian community in Etobicoke.
- **Vancouver:** small.

## Match-day rituals

- Turkish coffee and çay (tea).
- Kebab, döner, lahmacun.
- Shisha (nargile).
- "Turkiye! Turkiye!" chants.
- Drumming.
