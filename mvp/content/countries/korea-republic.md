# Korea Republic

The Red Devils (the supporter group, not the Belgian team). Massive supporter culture, Koreatown HQ in every host city. KBBQ, soju, drumming, "Daehan Minguk!" call and response.

## How Korea Republic fans show up

Korean BBQ restaurants (KBBQ), soju bars, and Korean-Town cafes are the home rooms. Wherever a Koreatown exists, that's the room. Drumming, red shirts, intense visual identity.

## Where to find the crowd

- **LA (Koreatown):** Quarters, Genwa, every KBBQ joint will have screens.
- **NYC (32nd St Koreatown):** Seoul BAP, Soju Haus, Pocha 32, Itaewon Pocha, Gunbae, Nogari, JuDam.
- **Atlanta (Duluth):** Koreatown North — Iron Age, multiple KBBQ.
- **Houston:** Spring Branch + Bellaire Korean restaurants.
- **Toronto:** Bloor Koreatown.
- **Vancouver:** Burnaby Korean restaurants.
- **Dallas:** Carrollton K-Town.
- **Seattle:** International District + Bellevue.

## Match-day rituals

- KBBQ (samgyeopsal, galbi).
- Soju shots and Cass beer.
- "Daehan Minguk! [clap clap clap-clap-clap]" — the iconic chant.
- Red shirts.
- Drums.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Son Heung-min likely captaining.
- Group stage with Czechia (3am at Estadio Akron) and Mexico at Estadio Akron.
- Mexico-Korea is one of the marquee fixtures of the tournament.

Red. Korea Republic shirt. Red devil headband.
