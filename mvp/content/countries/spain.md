# Spain

Reigning Euro 2024 champions. La Roja. Tapas-driven supporter culture in NYC and Miami. The Spanish-American expat scene is small but the cultural ties run deep through Latin American communities.

## How Spain fans show up

Tapas bars, Spanish restaurants, and Latin American restaurants generally pick up Spain matches. NYC's Boqueria locations are the most famous. Miami's Spanish-American community is significant.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Boqueria Soho, Boqueria Flatiron, Boqueria W40th, Tomiño Taberna Gallega, EZ Paella & Tapas.
- **Miami:** Casa Tua, La Esquina, Spanish-Latin restaurants in Brickell.
- **LA:** Bar Manuel, La Espanola Meats.
- **Atlanta:** spanning Spanish and Latin American restaurants.
- **Mexico City:** Spanish restaurants in Centro and Roma.

## Match-day rituals

- Tapas (jamón, croquetas, tortilla, gambas).
- Sangria, Rioja red wine, vermouth.
- "A por ellos!" chants.
- Knowledgeable, tactical conversation.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Reigning Euro champs.
- Lamine Yamal's first WC.
- Group stage with Cabo Verde at Mercedes-Benz.

## What to wear

Red and yellow. Spain shirt — current or "La Furia Roja" classic.

## Bring

Patience for tapas. Multiple small plates over a long match.
