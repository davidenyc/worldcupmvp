# Iraq

A historic return after years of qualification heartbreak. The Iraqi-American community in Detroit's Dearborn (largest Arab-American community in the US) and San Diego's El Cajon will be at full volume.

## How Iraq fans show up

Iraqi and Arab-American restaurants, cafés, and shisha lounges. Dearborn's Iraqi community is the largest in the US. Tea, dolma, masgouf (grilled river fish).

## Where to find the crowd

- **Detroit (Dearborn):** Multiple Iraqi-Arab restaurants on Warren Ave. Al-Saha, Sheeba.
- **San Diego (El Cajon):** Iraqi-Chaldean community — multiple restaurants.
- **Boston:** Allston Iraqi-Arab spots.
- **NYC:** Astoria Steinway Street Iraqi-Arab cafes.
- **Houston:** small but real.
- **Toronto:** small Iraqi-Canadian community in North York.

## Match-day rituals

- Tea, Turkish coffee, Iraqi tea (chai with cardamom).
- Masgouf, dolma, kibbeh.
- Shisha (hookah).
- Arabic flowing throughout.
