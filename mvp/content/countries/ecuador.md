# Ecuador

The Ecuadorian-American community is concentrated in Queens (especially Jackson Heights/Corona) and Newark. Loud, organized, and proud of every qualification.

## How Ecuador fans show up

Ecuadorian restaurants, particularly hornado and seco de chivo specialists, are the home rooms. The Roosevelt Avenue corridor in Queens has multiple options. Ecuadorian-Newark restaurants in the Iron-bound area.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC (Queens):** Hornado Ecuatoriano spots, Pollos a la Brasa Mario, Latin Cabana. Roosevelt Avenue corridor.
- **Newark:** Ecuadorian restaurants in the Ironbound.
- **Houston:** small but growing community.
- **DC area:** suburbs of Maryland and Virginia.
- **Mexico City:** Latin American expat scene in Roma.

## Match-day rituals

- Yellow shirts dominant.
- Hornado (roast pork), seco de chivo (goat stew).
- Pasillo and cumbia music.
- Drumming.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Group stage with Germany at MetLife — likely a group decider.
- Recent strong CONMEBOL form.

## What to wear

Yellow, blue, red. Ecuador shirt. Yellow dominant.

## Bring

Energy. The Ecuadorian rooms in Queens and Newark are some of the most underrated.
