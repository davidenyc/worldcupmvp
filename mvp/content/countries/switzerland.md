# Switzerland

The Nati. Multi-lingual supporter culture (German, French, Italian-Swiss). Small but well-organized diaspora. Often shared rooms with German fans.

## How Switzerland fans show up

Swiss expats often share German beer halls or Italian restaurants depending on linguistic background. Swiss cultural centers in major cities host watch parties for big matches.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Cafe Sabarsky (Austrian-Swiss crossover); German beer halls.
- **DC area:** Swiss-American cultural community in NoVa.
- **SF:** Swiss cultural center events.

## Match-day rituals

- Fondue (yes, even watching football).
