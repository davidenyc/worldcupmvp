# Portugal

Cristiano Ronaldo's likely last World Cup. The Portuguese-American diaspora is huge — Newark Ironbound is one of the largest Portuguese communities outside Portugal itself. Toronto's Little Portugal (Dundas West) is similarly historic.

## How Portugal fans show up

Portuguese restaurants, cafés (pastelarias), and supporter clubs are home rooms. The Ironbound in Newark is the densest concentration in the US. Toronto's Dundas West similarly.

## Where to find the crowd

- **Newark (Ironbound):** Iberia Tavern, Casa Vasca, multiple Portuguese restaurants and pastelarias.
- **Toronto (Little Portugal):** Café Diplomatico, Café Vienna, Casa do Alentejo.
- **NYC:** Taste of Portugal, Tasca Portuguesa.
- **Boston (Cambridge):** Casa Portugal, multiple Portuguese cafés.
- **Houston:** Portuguese community in Sugar Land.
- **SF (East Bay):** Portuguese community in Hayward and parts of Oakland.

## Match-day rituals

- Bifana (Portuguese pork sandwich) and pastel de nata.
- Vinho verde and Sagres beer.
- "Portugal! Portugal!" chants.
- Drumming.
- Cristiano Ronaldo iconography everywhere.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Ronaldo's last WC.
- Strong squad with Bruno Fernandes, Bernardo Silva, João Félix.
- Group stage with Congo DR at NRG Houston.

## What to wear

Green and red. Portugal shirt — Ronaldo back if appropriate. Red dominant.

Respect for Cristiano. The room is sentimental about him.
