# Colombia

Carnival energy. Colombia's supporter rooms are loud, dance-driven, and yellow. Champeta, vallenato, and reggaeton soundtrack the breaks.

## How Colombia fans show up

Colombian restaurants, areperias, and Latin nightclubs are the home rooms. The Jackson Heights (Queens) Colombian community is one of the densest in the US; Miami's Allappattah and Doral; Houston's east side. Yellow is everywhere.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC (Jackson Heights):** Pollos a la Brasa Mario, Areperias, multiple Colombian restaurants on Roosevelt Ave.
- **Miami (Allapattah, Doral):** Colombian restaurants, Latin Cafe.
- **Houston:** Cali-Colombian restaurants in southwest Houston.
- **Boston:** small but real community in East Boston and Lynn.
- **Mexico City:** Latin American expat scene in Roma.

## Match-day rituals

- Yellow shirts head-to-toe.
- Champeta and vallenato music breaks.
- Aguardiente shots if you're committing.
- Drumming, dancing, full bodies in motion.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Returning after missing 2022.
- James Rodríguez's veteran-led return.
- Strong 2024 Copa America final-runner-up form.

## What to wear

Yellow, blue, red. Colombia shirt. Yellow is mandatory.

## Bring

Energy and a willingness to dance through halftime.
