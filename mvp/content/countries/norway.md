# Norway

Returning to the World Cup after a long drought. Erling Haaland generation. The Norwegian-American community is concentrated in Minneapolis-St. Paul (Twin Cities) and Brooklyn's historic Bay Ridge.

## How Norway fans show up

Norwegian-American social clubs (Sons of Norway lodges), Scandinavian restaurants, and Lutheran church halls are surprising home rooms. Twin Cities is the densest concentration.

## Where to find the crowd

- **Minneapolis (proxy):** Sons of Norway lodges, Scandinavian Hjem, FIKA Cafe.
- **NYC (Bay Ridge, Brooklyn):** historic Norwegian community; Norwegian American Hospital is a landmark. Some Norwegian-American restaurants remain.
- **Seattle (Ballard):** Scandinavian heritage neighborhood; some Norwegian spots.
- **Boston:** small.

## Match-day rituals

- Aquavit shots if you commit.
- Lutefisk if you really commit.
- Knäckebröd.
- Quiet, knowledgeable supporter base.
- Haaland chants ramping up as the tournament progresses.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Erling Haaland's first World Cup.
- Group stage with Senegal at MetLife.

Red, white, blue (with Nordic cross). Norway shirt. Or red flannel for the Sons of Norway aesthetic.
