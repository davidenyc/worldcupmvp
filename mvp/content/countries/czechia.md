# Czechia

Czech beer culture provides natural home rooms. Smaller diaspora than the football culture suggests, but Czech beer halls are everywhere.

## How Czechia fans show up

Czech beer halls and Czech-American social clubs are the home base. Bohemian Hall & Beer Garden in Astoria (NYC) is the largest and most famous Czech beer garden in the US.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** Bohemian Hall & Beer Garden (Astoria). The room.
- **Chicago:** Czech-American clubs in suburbs.
- **Cleveland:** historic Czech community.
- **Toronto:** Bohemian Cafe, Bratislava Cafe (Slovak-Czech crossover).
- **Atlanta (host):** smaller scene; default to German halls.

## Match-day rituals

- Pilsner Urquell on tap. Required.
- Czech sausages and bread dumplings.
- Quiet but knowledgeable crowd.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

- Returning to WC after several missed cycles.
- Group stage with South Africa, Korea Republic.

## What to wear

Red, blue, white. Czechia shirt or scarf.

## Bring

Appreciation for proper Czech pilsner. The beer is the point.
