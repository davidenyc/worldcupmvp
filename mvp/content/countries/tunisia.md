# Tunisia

The Eagles of Carthage. North African supporter culture. Tunisian-American community small in NA — Montreal has the largest Tunisian diaspora. Often shared rooms with Algerian and Moroccan fans.

## How Tunisia fans show up

North African cafés and Maghreb restaurants. Shared rooms with Algeria and Morocco fans for African solidarity matches.

## Where to find the crowd

- **NYC:** North African cafés in Astoria.
- **Montreal (proxy):** Plateau and Côte-des-Neiges Tunisian-Maghreb restaurants.
- **DC area:** small Tunisian-American community.

## Match-day rituals

- Mint tea and Maghreb pastries.
- Couscous and tagine.
- Shisha.
- Arabic, French, Berber flowing.

## Tournament storylines (2026)

Red and white. Tunisia shirt.
