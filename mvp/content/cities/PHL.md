# Philadelphia — what watch parties feel like here

Lincoln Financial Field hosts six matches. Philadelphia has older European immigrant communities (Italian, Irish, Polish — none playing this WC) and growing newer ones (Mexican, Liberian, Haitian, Korean, Indian). The Italian Market and South Philly cantinas are the surprise rooms.

## How to think about Philly

Pick the country, then the part of the city. Center City and University City have generic-good sports bars. South Philly (Italian Market, Mexican community) has the Mexican rooms. Olney and the Northeast have West African and Caribbean communities. Cherry Hill NJ holds the larger Indian and Jewish communities.

## Where each crowd lives

- **USA** — Center City pubs, Xfinity Live (stadium-adjacent), Misconduct Tavern, The Field House.
- **Mexico** — South Philly (9th St / Italian Market). Mexican cantinas have grown in volume. El Compadre, Las Cazuelas.
- **England / Scotland** — Fadó (closed in some cities, check), The Dark Horse, The Plough & the Stars. Solid UK-pub scene.
- **Ireland** (not playing) — Irish-American pubs everywhere; default to nearest neighbor.
- **Korea Republic** — Cheltenham, Elkins Park, parts of Old City. Korean restaurants and KBBQ.
- **Brazil** — small but real. Bossa Nova-style spots in Center City and Manayunk.
- **Haiti** — Olney, Logan, Northeast Philly. Haitian restaurants.
- **Liberia / West African** — Southwest Philly, Upper Darby. Senegalese, Ghanaian, Liberian communities concentrated.
- **Senegal / Ghana** — Southwest Philly. Restaurants and supporter clubs. Tight scene.
- **Portugal** — small; Cherry Hill NJ has spots.
- **Germany** — limited; Brauhaus Schmitz (South Philly) is the spot.
- **Japan** — sparse; Morimoto, izakaya scene small.
- **Argentina** — small Argentine restaurants in Center City and Manayunk.
- **Spain** — Amada, Tinto.
- **IR Iran** — Persian community in suburbs (Cherry Hill, Wynnewood).
- **Egypt** — small but growing Northeast Philly scene.

## Neighborhoods worth knowing

- **South Philly / Italian Market** — Mexican supporter rooms. The Italian-American bars now show every match (including Mexico, since Italy isn't playing).
- **Center City / Fishtown / Northern Liberties** — generic sports bars.
- **Southwest Philly / Upper Darby** — West African (Senegal, Ghana, Liberia, Cote d'Ivoire).
- **Northeast Philly / Olney** — Haitian, Caribbean.
- **Cheltenham / Elkins Park** — Korean.
- **Cherry Hill NJ (15 min east)** — Indian, Jewish, Persian.

## Match-day logistics

- **Lincoln Financial Field:** Broad Street Line subway directly to stadium complex. Easy.
- **From Center City to LFF:** 15 min by subway.
- **Pre-match:** Xfinity Live and stadium-adjacent bars fill 2 hours pre-kickoff.
- **Post-match:** South Philly bars fill from match-end onward.

## Weather

June/July: hot and humid, 80–90°F. Patios brutal afternoon. AC mandatory.

## Booking advice

- USA matches: Center City pubs reserve.
- Mexico matches: South Philly cantinas walk-in only; arrive early or go to a sit-down restaurant that reserves.
- West African matches: Southwest Philly restaurants don't reserve; arrive early.
- Korea matches: Cheltenham KBBQ reserves.

## Submit a venue

Liberian, Sierra Leonean, Haitian, Bangladeshi rooms in Olney and SW Philly underrepresented.
