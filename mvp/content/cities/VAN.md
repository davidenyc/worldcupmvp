# Vancouver — what watch parties feel like here

BC Place hosts six matches including Canada-Qatar. Vancouver's strength is its East and South Asian communities (Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Filipino, Punjabi/Indian — the last two not playing but enormous proxy crowds), Persian community in North Vancouver, and a growing Latin American community in Surrey and the Lower Mainland.

## How to think about Vancouver

The city is small and walkable. Pick the country, take the SkyTrain. Persian goes to North Vancouver (largest Persian community in Canada). Korean to Burnaby and Coquitlam. Latin American to Surrey.

## Where each crowd lives

- **Canada** — Yaletown, Gastown, BC Place-adjacent. The Pint, Library Square, Mahony & Sons.
- **Korea Republic** — Burnaby (Lougheed Town Centre area), Coquitlam, downtown. KBBQ rooms.
- **Japan** — downtown (Robson Street), Richmond. Izakayas: Hapa Izakaya, Suika.
- **China / Hong Kong** (not playing) — Richmond. Hong Kong cafes will show neighboring Asian matches.
- **Philippines** (not playing — proxy) — Surrey, Richmond. Will show Asian matches.
- **Mexico** — Surrey, parts of East Vancouver. Cantinas. La Taqueria, Sal y Limón.
- **Brazil** — small but real. Buena Vibra, Brazilian-Canadian community in West End.
- **Argentina** — sparse.
- **IR Iran** — North Vancouver (largest Persian community in Canada). Persian restaurants and tea houses on Lonsdale.
- **Senegal / Ghana** — small communities in East Vancouver and Surrey.
- **Australia** (proxy for Pacific) — sizable Aussie expat community; Granville Street pubs.
- **England / Scotland** — Library Square, The Cambie, Mahony & Sons. Solid UK-pub scene.
- **Germany** — German Club (Granville), Bavarian Pavilion.
- **Saudi Arabia / Jordan** — small Arab community in Burnaby and Surrey.
- **Punjabi (India — not playing — proxy)** — Punjabi Market (49th & Main), Surrey. Massive community will show neighbors.

## Neighborhoods worth knowing

- **North Vancouver (Lonsdale)** — Persian community. IR Iran matches.
- **Burnaby (Korean)** — Korea matches.
- **Richmond** — Chinese, Japanese, Filipino. Asian matches.
- **Surrey** — Punjabi, South Asian, Filipino, growing Mexican.
- **East Vancouver / Commercial Drive** — Italian (not playing), Mexican, generic.
- **Yaletown / Gastown** — Canada match-day hub.

## Match-day logistics

- **BC Place:** Stadium-Chinatown SkyTrain station. Steps from the stadium. Easy.
- **Pre-match:** Yaletown and Gastown fill for Canada matches.
- **From North Van to BC Place:** SeaBus + SkyTrain, 30 min.

## Weather

June/July: 65–75°F, mostly sunny, occasional showers. Patios usable.

## Booking advice

- Canada matches: Yaletown/Gastown reserve.
- Korea matches: Burnaby KBBQ reserves.
- IRN matches: North Van Persian restaurants reserve for groups; walk-in for tea houses.

## Submit a venue

Salvadoran, Honduran, Eritrean rooms in Surrey and East Van underrepresented.
