# Atlanta — what watch parties feel like here

Mercedes-Benz Stadium hosts seven matches. Atlanta has a deep Atlanta United supporter culture, large Latin American (especially Mexican) communities in Buford Highway and Smyrna, growing West African and Ethiopian communities, and Korean-American suburbs in Duluth/Doraville.

## How to think about ATL

Atlanta United fans set the bar for soccer-aware bars in the South. Downtown for stadium-adjacent matches; Buford Highway corridor for Latin American, Korean, Vietnamese; East Atlanta and Decatur for African; Smyrna and Marietta for Mexican.

## Where each crowd lives

- **USA** — Midtown, Old Fourth Ward, Buckhead. Brewhouse Cafe (Little Five Points), Hudson Grille, Park Bar.
- **Mexico** — Buford Highway, Smyrna, Marietta. Cantinas, taco shops with TVs. Plaza Fiesta has multiple options.
- **Argentina** — small. Argentine restaurants in Buckhead and Marietta.
- **Brazil** — small. Brazilian churrascarias and a few Brazilian-American spots.
- **England / Scotland** — Brewhouse Cafe, Fadó (if still open), Jock's & Jill's.
- **Germany** — Der Biergarten, Steinhaus.
- **Korea Republic** — Duluth/Doraville. Korea-Town Atlanta. KBBQ rooms with screens.
- **Japan** — Little Tokyo around Spring/Buford. Limited.
- **Vietnam** (not playing — proxy) — Buford Highway pho spots show LATAM matches sometimes.
- **Senegal / Ghana / West African** — East Atlanta, Decatur, Lithonia. Tight community.
- **Ethiopia / Eritrea** (not playing — proxy) — Clarkston (the most diverse square mile in the US). Will show neighboring African nations.
- **South Africa** — small; Buckhead and Brookhaven.
- **IR Iran** — small; Sandy Springs.
- **Egypt** — small but growing; Sandy Springs.

## Neighborhoods worth knowing

- **Buford Highway corridor** — diverse Asian/Latin American food scene. Multiple flag bars and restaurants.
- **Smyrna / Marietta** — large Mexican community.
- **Duluth / Doraville (Korea-Town)** — Korean.
- **Clarkston** — refugee-resettlement community, hugely diverse, hosts smaller national groups.
- **East Atlanta / Decatur** — West African, Ethiopian, generic-good sports bars.

## Match-day logistics

- **Mercedes-Benz Stadium:** MARTA to GWCC/CNN Center, walk to stadium. Easy.
- **From Buford Highway to MBS:** 25 min drive.
- **Pre-match:** Centennial Park area fills.

## Weather

June/July: hot, humid, 85–95°F, afternoon thunderstorms.

## Booking advice

- USA matches: Midtown sports bars reserve.
- Mexico matches: Buford Highway and Smyrna cantinas walk-in.
- Korea matches: Duluth KBBQ reserves.
- West African matches: small restaurants don't reserve; arrive early.

## Submit a venue

Ethiopian, Eritrean (despite not playing — important proxy rooms), Liberian, Salvadoran rooms underrepresented.
