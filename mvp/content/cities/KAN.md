# Kansas City — what watch parties feel like here

Arrowhead hosts five matches. KC has a vibrant Mexican and Latin American community (Westside especially), a growing African community in suburbs, and a strong USA fan base from Sporting KC supporter culture. Smaller scene than coastal hosts, but tight.

## How to think about KC

KC is compact. Most fans converge on Power & Light District for marquee matches and on the Westside (Mexican-American) for Mexico/LATAM matches. Sporting KC supporter culture means soccer-aware bars exist.

## Where each crowd lives

- **USA** — Power & Light District, Westport. Buzzard Beach, Tower Tavern, Brewery Emperial. Sporting KC fans concentrated.
- **Mexico** — Westside KC, Argentine District, parts of KCK. Cantinas. Carmen's, El Camino Real.
- **Argentina / Latin American** — Argentine District (yes, that's the name) west of downtown.
- **England / Scotland** — McCoy's, Browne's Irish Marketplace (mostly Irish-American but show neighbors).
- **Korea Republic** — Overland Park (KS) suburbs. Limited but present.
- **Senegal / West African** — small but growing community in KCK and northeast suburbs.
- **Germany** — Bier Station (Waldo), Strang Hall.
- **Japan** — sparse; small ramen scene.
- **Brazil** — Texas de Brazil-style churrascarias; small.

## Neighborhoods worth knowing

- **Power & Light District** — downtown KC sports/entertainment district. USA matches here.
- **Westside KC** — Mexican supporter rooms.
- **Argentine District (KCK)** — historic Latin American neighborhood.
- **Westport** — generic sports bars, college-age crowd.
- **Overland Park (KS)** — Korean, Indian, Vietnamese communities.

## Match-day logistics

- **Arrowhead Stadium:** drive — no transit. 15 min from downtown. $40+ parking. Tailgating culture from NFL crowds.
- **From P&L District:** 20 min drive to Arrowhead.
- **Pre-match:** P&L District is the hub.

## Weather

June/July: 85–95°F, very humid, afternoon thunderstorms. Outdoor venues hit-or-miss.

## Booking advice

- USA at Arrowhead: P&L bars reserve.
- Mexico-Argentina or Argentina marquee: Westside cantinas walk-in; or reserve at sit-down restaurants.

## Submit a venue

Salvadoran, Honduran, Sudanese rooms underrepresented.
