# Guadalajara — what watch parties feel like here

Estadio Akron in Zapopan hosts five matches including Mexico-Korea Republic. Guadalajara is the heart of Mexican football culture (Chivas, Atlas) and the cantinas and birria spots will be standing-room for any Mexico match.

## How to think about GDL

Mexico matches are city-wide events. Every cantina, every taquería, every birria spot with a screen will be packed. For non-Mexico matches, head to Chapultepec/Lafayette (cosmopolitan), Providencia (affluent expat-aware), or Andares (mall-adjacent sports bars).

## Where each crowd lives

- **Mexico** — everywhere. Birrierías in Centro and Tlaquepaque. Cantinas. Plaza Liberación for any official fan zone.
- **Korea Republic** — small but real expat community; Asian fusion spots in Chapultepec and Andares.
- **USA / Canada expats** — Chapultepec, Providencia. American-style sports bars exist (Hooters Plaza Patria, Tomatlán).
- **Argentina** — small Argentine community; parrillas in Providencia.
- **Spain** — Spanish cultural ties run deep; Spanish-themed restaurants throughout Centro and Lafayette.
- **France** — small expat scene in Lafayette and Chapultepec.
- **Brazil** — sparse.
- **England** — Sherlock Holmes Pub (Centro), British-themed spots.
- **Germany** — small; Lonchería Bavaria-adjacent spots.
- **Czechia** — sparse but Czech beer halls have appeared.

## Neighborhoods worth knowing

- **Chapultepec / Lafayette** — bohemian, cosmopolitan, multi-cultural sports bars.
- **Providencia / Andares** — affluent, sports-bar driven.
- **Centro Histórico** — old-school cantinas. Best for Mexico match atmosphere.
- **Tlaquepaque** — artisan/tourist district; cantinas and pulquerías.
- **Zapopan (Andares-adjacent)** — Estadio Akron neighborhood.

## Match-day logistics

- **Estadio Akron:** Uber to Zapopan. ~30 min from Centro. Matchday transit shuttles run.
- **Pre-match:** Andares-area sports bars fill. Centro fills 2 hours before any Mexico match.

## Weather

June/July GDL: 75–85°F, rainy season. Afternoon thunderstorms common. Outdoor patios hit-or-miss.

## Booking advice

- Mexico matches: reserve at Andares mall sports bars. Walk-in only at Centro cantinas.
- Korea-Czechia (3am local): only late-night spots open. Asian fusion in Andares might run special hours.

## Submit a venue

Birrierías and pulquerías in Centro and Tlaquepaque underrepresented. Submit them.
