# GameDay Map — One-Pager

**Find your World Cup watch party.**
17 host cities. 48 nations. Every bar and restaurant that matters.

---

## What

A fan-first web app to find the right place to watch every match of FIFA World Cup 2026. Filter by country supporter base, atmosphere, capacity, neighborhood, and reservation availability. Curated venues. Real-time matches. Group booking. Built before kickoff, used through the final.

## Why now

- 48 teams. 104 matches. 17 cities across 3 countries. The largest tournament ever.
- Most fans will watch from a bar. Existing tools dump them into "sports bars near me." Wrong question.
- Right question: where is my country's crowd, at this match, in this neighborhood, with seats?

## How it works

1. Pick your city. Pick your country (or any of 48).
2. See every venue tagged for your crowd — sports bars, cultural restaurants, supporter clubs, fan fest.
3. Filter by atmosphere, capacity, price, reservations, near-me, open-now.
4. Save it, share it, reserve it, build a group around it.

## Tiers

- **Free** — browse 17 cities, filter 2 countries, save 5 venues.
- **Fan Pass · $4.99/mo** — all 48 country filters, unlimited saves, reservation requests, advanced filters, match-day alerts. Most fans pick this.
- **Supporter Elite · $12.99/mo** — everything in Fan Pass + push notifications, Venue Concierge (a real human handles your reservations), group booking, partner discounts.

7-day refund. One-click cancel. No ads. No data sales.
