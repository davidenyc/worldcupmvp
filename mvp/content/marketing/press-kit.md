## What it is, in one minute

GameDay Map (gamedaymap.com) is a web app that helps fans find the right place to watch every match of FIFA World Cup 2026 across the 17 host cities. It's not a generic bar finder. It's a fan-first map where every venue is tagged by the country supporters who actually show up there, the atmosphere on match day, capacity, and reservation availability. Free for casual fans; Fan Pass ($4.99/mo) and Supporter Elite ($12.99/mo) unlock advanced filters, reservation requests, group booking, and push alerts.

## Why it matters

The 2026 World Cup is the largest ever — 48 teams, 104 matches, three host countries, 17 cities. Most fans will watch from a bar, restaurant, or supporter hangout in a city they don't know well. Existing tools treat that as a search problem ("sports bars near me") instead of a fan problem ("where will my country's crowd be on Tuesday at 3pm"). GameDay Map fixes that.
