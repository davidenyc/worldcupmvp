# Venue Partner Outreach Pack

For: bar and restaurant owners, supporter club leads, and hospitality groups in any of the 17 host cities.

Goal: get them to (1) claim their listing and (2) book a 10-minute call about Featured placement during the tournament.

Editor: keep it short. Owners are busy and skeptical.

---

## Pricing tiers (Featured placements)

- **Standard listing** — Free. Claim, edit, post hours, photos, matches, reservation link. ~80% of revenue value comes from this — most fans never click past the listing.
- **Featured · $99/mo** — Top of city map results. Featured pill. Cross-promotion in the matching country guide and city editorial. 5 spots per neighborhood.
- **Marquee Match · $499 one-time** — Hot Spot badge for a specific match (e.g. Brazil vs Morocco, MetLife). One marquee per match per city. Includes match alert email mention to fans following that match.
- **Tournament Sponsor · custom** — City-wide co-marketing, group booking pipeline, kit affiliate revshare, custom landing page. Email partners@gamedaymap.com.

All paid tiers come with a 30-day satisfaction guarantee — if your bar isn't busier on Featured days, we refund.

## Objection handling

**"We don't need another listing site."**
> Fair. Most listing sites dump fans into "sports bars near me" — we filter by country, capacity, atmosphere. The fan looking for a Brazil bar finds you specifically. Free claim takes 90 seconds and you can leave Featured for later if it's not a fit.

**"$99/mo is too much."**
> Two slots in a 200-capacity room on a marquee match covers the whole month. We'll refund inside 30 days if it doesn't move. Or stay on the free listing — it works either way.

**"I don't have time to manage another platform."**
> The free claim takes 90 seconds. If you go Featured, you get a direct line to me — no portal to learn. I handle the platform, you run the bar.

**"Why are you better than Yelp/Google?"**
> Different job. Yelp and Google answer "where can I eat?" We answer "where is my crowd watching this match?" Fans use both for different things. We're a complement, not a replacement.

**"Will fans actually use this?"**
> We're going into the tournament with curated coverage of all 17 host cities and 48 nations. Most listing tools have neither. We've already had {{n_views}} views on your listing alone. Match days will multiply that.

**"I want to see traffic data first."**
> Email me at {{sender_email}} from your verified venue email. I'll send you a one-page report on your listing's last 30 days — views, clicks-to-directions, clicks-to-reserve. No commitment.
